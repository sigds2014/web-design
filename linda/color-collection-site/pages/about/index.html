Color-based Mood Board Inspiration for Designers

Let me start by saying that I love color and I love mood boards. It doesn't matter if I'm starting a project, feeling creatively blocked, or just plain bored—looking at mood boards gets my creative juices flowing and inspires me to create or think about things in new ways.

For that reason, I wanted to create a collection of mood boards for designers and artistic types that share my love for them. These mood boards are based on a color palette and the color psychology surrounding a particular type of color. In this way, other designers can use this site to be “inspired by color,” so to speak, and to find inspiration in unique color combinations and imagery based on color feelings.
