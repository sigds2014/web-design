About

Poster Farm was founded in the summer of 2014 by Nate Moore (natemoo.re) at the Rhode Island School of Design.

Poster Farm endevours to highlight outstanding work in the gig poster scene and artists who are constantly innovating. Each day, the home page features a new poster—each week, a new artist or group of artists. Please see the archives for previous featured posters and artists.

If you have any questions, would like to see your work featured, or have any reason to get in touch, please don’t hesitate to drop us a line below.
