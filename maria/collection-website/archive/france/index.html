France

Think of France, of strolling past the Eiffel Tower as you hold a baguette under your arm, going in no particular direction down the enchanting streets of Paris. Think of the nostalgic dream of Woody Allen's film "Midnight in Paris". Think also of charming Provence, where fields of lavender hypnotize each traveler's heart. Regardless, I guarantee you will think of romance, of beauty, of tradition, and whether you want to or not, of art.

Throughout history, France has established itself as the destination of innovation and culture, a place to which artists flocked from all corners of the world in order to partake in the wondrous, artistic rhythm of life there.
