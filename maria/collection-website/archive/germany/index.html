Germany

Consider Germany, a majestic country at the heart of Europe where beer is served almost everwhere and the yucky American hotdogs are replaced by delicious German bratwurst variations. In thinking of Germany you may think of its quiant, beautiful towns as well as its fairytale castles. You may also remember the country's history, heavily influenced by war. Yet today, Germany is in many ways the financial metropolis of Europe, with Berlin as its capital. Considering Germany, though, you will not be able to avoid culture. You see, beneath Germany's tough, businessy front, is art.

Art in the Deutschland plays an exquisite role as the means which shows the thoughts and the emotions behind the strong nation. You may tour some of Germany's underlying personalities here:

In Berlin and Munich: Gernäldegalerie, Berlinische Galerie, and Alte Pinakothek, Schackgalerie

Elsewhere: Kunsthalle in Hamburg
