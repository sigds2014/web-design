Spain

Viva España, the lively country towards the south of Europe where rhythm, passion, and flavor come together ot form an incredibly dynamic nation. Thinking of Spain, you will most likely think of its two most famous cities Mardid and Barcelona. Your belly will delight at the idea of Paella, while your appetites for culture will land you in the bull fighting ring or in a flamenco bar. Yet in imagining España you will also think to its astounding spaces such as the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, Parque de Retiro in Madrid, the ancient city of Toledo, and so many more!

No matter how hard you may try, though, you will not be able to avoid the immense presence of art in Spain. The Spanish lust for life shows through the vivacious colors, motives, and creativity of its art. Here is where to immerse yourself in Spain:

In Madrid and Barcelona: Museo Nacional del Prado, Real Academia de Bellas Artes, and Picasso Museum, Joan Miró Foundation
