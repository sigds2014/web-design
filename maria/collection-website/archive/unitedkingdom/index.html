United Kingdom

Cheerio to Great Britain, the great nation of words, ranging from Shakespeare's poetry to Charles Dickens' realistic prose, to the lyrics of such bands as The Beatles. When wandering about England, you may also think of tea parties, and fish and chips at pubs. Nevetheless, you cannot think of the UK without London, without letting yourself dash past the London Eye and Big Ben on a double-decker bus, fantasizing about royalty as you pass the Buckingham palace. And then there is Oxford and Cambdrige, and the beautiful England countryside.

Amongst all of its diverse contributions, one of the largest is without a doubt art. Ranging from the most traditional royal portraits to the punk inspired works whose influence is prevalent in modern art today. When touring UK, stop by here:

In London:National Gallery, Tate Modern, British Museum

Elsewhere:Ashmolean Museum in Oxford
