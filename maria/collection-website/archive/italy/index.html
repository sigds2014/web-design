Italy

Imagine Italy, just like Audrey Hepburn had in "Roman Holiday" as she rode along Rome's famous sights on a vespa. Whether you think of the delicious food, or the rich history evident in monuments as the Colosseum or the Vatican, you are undeniably swayed by the loud, romantic, adventurous rhythm of the country. And when you think it couldn't get any better, there is Florence, and Venice, and the Amalfi Coast!

However, no matter where you travel to in Italy, you will be unceasingly reminded of art, art, art, art everywhere! You will feel art's colors in Italy's personality in each view, each step, each bite, you will take. So as you indulge in all of Italy has to offer, take a taste of these museums:

In Rome: The Vatican, Palazzo Altemps, Trevi Fountain

In Florence and Milan: Uffizi Gallery, Florence Academy of Fine Arts, and church of Santa Maria delle Grazie (Leonardo's last supper)
