Russian Federation

Reflect on Russia, amongst the largest countries in the world, geographically as well as historically and culturally. When thinking of the Russian Federation today, your thoughts may drift to its political influence, yet allow yourself to think instead of its enormous cultural influence through such texts as Crime and Punishment by Dostoyevsky and the enchanting music of the Nutcracker by Tchaikovsky. Delve farther, imagining the glorious Red Square in Moscow, and the beautiful city of St. Petersburg.

Russia is known for many things, its famous nesting dolls, its folk dances, its beet soup, but also its art. Russian art is incredibly diverse, bringing history and culture to the canvas in powerful, innovative, and raw brush strokes. If you wish to see the true colors of Russia, go here:

In Moscow: Tretyakov State Gallery, The Pushkin State Museum of Fine Arts, Moscow Museum of Modern Art

In St. Petersburg: State Hermitage Museum, Russian Museum
