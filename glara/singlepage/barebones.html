Master of Practicality: Marni Fog Derby

at la garconne
its relative

I was once debating between black, navy, and white shoes at a sample sale when a woman chimed in, “Get the white one!” to which other people started joining in, “The white one!” “The white one!” Why the sudden charm?

For times without such group support, this taupe, foggy-white Derby is minimalist in approach while still easy to digest. Made of three basic elements, black, white, and gold, I would be tempted to throw off a long black dress with this dark-bordered beauty, if not wear as rainboots, if I can stand to defy its inherent practicality.
